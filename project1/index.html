System Modules

Text Analysis

NLP-based inspection of suspicious words, emotional tone, and sensational claims that often appear in fake narratives.

Image Analysis

CNN and visual forensics look for blending artifacts, skin tone mismatch, edge anomalies, and face tampering traces.

Video Analysis

Frame-level and temporal checks identify lip-sync mismatch, unnatural motion, and inconsistent illumination across frames.
